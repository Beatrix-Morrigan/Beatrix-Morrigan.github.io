Dirty Little Machine

from Beatrix Morrigan, Digital Artificer

this website is named after a suction-based DIY tool used by underground abortionists in the early 1970s. they decided to take the means of reproductive choice into their own hands, liberating people with ovaries from forced pregnancy before the medical establishment was even considering making such services available. the abortionists who made these devices called it the Del-Em, after a scornful doctor referred to it as "that dirty little machine".
you can learn more about the original Del-Em in
  • "Part Two: Abortion and its Defenders: From Weimar to the Bay" by Margaret Killjoy for Cool Zone Media on January 30 2024
  • "The Resurgence of the Abortion Underground" by Jessica Bruder for The Atlantic's The Experiment Podcast on April 22 2022
