  • Interview with Ernesto Aguilar of the Anarchist People of Color (APOC) by The Female Species on June 28 2003. Hosted on the Colours of Resistance Archive.

  • Shinin’ the Lite on White, Part One: White Privilege by Sharon Martinas. Hosted on the Colours of Resistance Archive.
  • 25 ways to tokenize or alienate a non-white person around you (or, 25 examples of the racism we witness on a regular basis) by basil, billie, qwo-li, jenn and colin. Hosted on the Colours of Resistance Archive.

  • Tools for White Guys who are Working for Social Change and other people socialized in a society based on domination by Chris Crass. Hosted on the Colours of Resistance Archive.
