What's the Takeaway Here?

In my professional and personal opinion, "AI" hasn't yet been developed into a rigorous or sustainable tool. Perhaps it *could* be, with more research and refinement, but I can't recommend or engage with its current iteration, as I understand that it would be deleterious to the quality of my work and my comprehension of the systems my work pertains to, on top of my ethical objections to its current form.
